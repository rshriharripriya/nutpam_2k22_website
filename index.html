About Nutpam

Nutpam 2k22 is created with the theme of “The Mighty Ocean”. With a simple, yet intriguing theme, we are obliged to create an indulging knowledge pool for all the engineers, just like the mighty ocean with exciting and innovative events designed to be fun, entertaining and hectic at the same time. Nutpam 2k22 is an in-person event one-day tech carnival conducted by Sri Sairam Institute of Technology with the sole motive of bringing the best out of the young minds and pave way to build the world with elegant and magnificent engineering and technologies.

About Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is accredited by NBA, AICTE and has acquired Autonomous status under Anna University, Chennai. The alacrity of the students to learn makes it easier for the Department of Computer Science and Engineering to produce top-notch Engineers. CSE Department is equipped with 36 well experienced faculty and 9 supporting staff. The Department has a PG course in “ME-Big Data Analytics”. Every year the Department produces at least 10 rank holders. The Department has the Centre of Excellences for Red Hat, Cisco, Oracle, Sales force, VMware, Data Science and Big Data Analytics. The students of CSE were placed in top MNC companies like TCS, WIPRO, Inautix, CTS, ZOHO, L & T InfoTech, Amazon and so on.
