About Nutpam

The Department of CSE is accredited by NBA,AICTE and has acquired Autonomous status under Anna University, Chennai.Every year Department produces at least 10 rank holders and has Centre of Excellences for Red Hat, Cisco, Oracle, Salesforce, VMWare & Data Science and Big Data Analytics.The students of CSE were placed in top MNC companies like TCS,WIPRO,CTS,ZOHO,Amazon and so on.The Department has signed MoU's with ICTACT, Red Hat Academy, Cisco Academy, Oracle Academy, SANDS India Pvt. Ltd., EPR Labs, IBM Rational Seed, Network Bulls Pvt.Ltd., and CSN Systems. Our students are active members of CSI,ISTE, SIESRP and IEI.
