Astra's Gambit

Valorant 5v5 Gaming Tournament

Rules & Guidelines

  • Toxic behaviour is not entertained and may lead to direct elimination.
  • Substitute players is not mandatory.
  • Tactical timeouts will be available for each side once per match.

Qualifiers: - Every match will be an elimination round of best of 1. - Coin tosses will be performed to decide to choose the map and sides before each match. - Each team can ban a map before the starting of the match. - Facture map is out by default in the map pool. Finals: - Finals will be a best of 3. - Each team gets to ban and pick one map each and the last map by coin toss

  • Event Date: 1st November 2022
  • Event Time: 7 PM IST to 12 AM IST
