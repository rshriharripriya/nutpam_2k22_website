Fountain of youth

A Treasure hunting event (Non-Technical)With the theme of “The mighty ocean”, The idea of hunting for hidden treasures has always been exciting and adventurous. In Fountain of Youth, participants work in small teams to find the treasure with the help of given clues.

Rules & Guidelines

  • This is a group event of two participants
  • Clues will be given to the participants,By using the given clues they have to reach the destination
  • The one who fails to identify the clues will be eliminated
  • Communication or collaboration with other teams will lead to disqualification of both the teams.
  • Venue : Sri Sairam Institute of Technology Campus
  • Timings :10.30-2.30(tentative)
